Our Purpose

At Tiny Heart , our purpose is to provide a safe, loving, and nurturing environment for orphaned and vulnerable children. We aim to give every child a sense of belonging and the opportunities they need to grow, learn, and thrive.

Mission

Our mission is to empower children through education, care, and emotional support, helping them become self-reliant and confident individuals who contribute positively to society.

Vision

We envision a world where every child has the opportunity to lead a fulfilling life, regardless of their circumstances. Tiny Heart Orphanage strives to be a beacon of hope for children in need.

Our Goals

  • Provide a safe and secure shelter for orphaned children.
  • Facilitate access to quality education and healthcare.
  • Offer emotional and psychological support.
  • Promote adoption and foster care awareness.
  • Equip children with life skills for a bright future.

Get Involved

Your support can change lives. Join us in our mission by donating, volunteering, or spreading the word. Together, we can make a difference.
